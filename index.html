About

Greetings, and welcome to my website. I am a second-year PhD student in Daniel Lowd's Machine Learning Group at the University of Oregon, where I research artificial intelligence. I am mostly focused on the field of neuro-symbolic AI, which attempts to integrate neural networks and symbolic systems as a pathway towards improved reasoning in intelligence systems. I feel inspired by any work that navigates the crazy expanse of consciousness and seeks to understand the mechanisms of the mind.

I spend most of my time out in nature when I am not working. I like mountaineering, downhill mountain biking and backpacking around the wilderness that surrounds Eugene.
